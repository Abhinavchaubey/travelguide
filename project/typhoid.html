Typhoid

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Weakness and fatigue
  • Abdominal pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Diarrhea or constipation
  • Skin rashes

Precautions

  • Drink only boiled or purified water
  • Wash hands regularly
  • Eat well-cooked food
  • Get vaccinated if at risk

Medicines

  • Ciprofloxacin
  • Azithromycin
  • Ceftriaxone
