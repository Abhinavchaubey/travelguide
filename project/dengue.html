Dengue

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe headaches
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Skin rashes
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting

Precautions

  • Use mosquito repellents
  • Wear long-sleeved clothes
  • Keep surroundings clean
  • Avoid stagnant water

Medicines

  • Paracetamol (for fever)
  • ORS (for dehydration)
  • Acetaminophen (for pain relief)
  • Fluids and electrolytes
