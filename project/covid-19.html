COVID-19

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue

Precautions

  • Wear a mask in crowded places
  • Maintain social distancing
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Get vaccinated

Medicines

  • Paracetamol
  • Azithromycin
  • Vitamin C and D supplements
  • Antiviral medications as prescribed
