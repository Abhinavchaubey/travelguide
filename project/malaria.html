Malaria

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Chills and sweating
  • Headaches
  • Muscle pain
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting

Precautions

  • Use mosquito nets
  • Apply mosquito repellents
  • Avoid stagnant water
  • Wear protective clothing

Medicines

  • Chloroquine
  • Artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT)
  • Mefloquine
  • Doxycycline
