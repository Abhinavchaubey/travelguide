Tuberculosis (TB)

Symptoms

  • Persistent cough (more than 3 weeks)
  • Chest pain
  • Unintentional weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Fever and night sweats
  • Loss of appetite

Precautions

  • Avoid close contact with TB patients
  • Wear a mask in crowded areas
  • Maintain proper hygiene
  • Get vaccinated (BCG vaccine)

Medicines

  • Isoniazid
  • Rifampin
  • Ethambutol
  • Pyrazinamide
