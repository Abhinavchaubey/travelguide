Cholera

Symptoms

  • Severe watery diarrhea
  • Dehydration
  • Low blood pressure
  • Muscle cramps
  • Nausea and vomiting

Precautions

  • Drink only clean or boiled water
  • Maintain proper sanitation
  • Avoid raw or undercooked food
  • Practice good hand hygiene

Medicines

  • Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS)
  • Azithromycin
  • Doxycycline
